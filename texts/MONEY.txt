In the wake of the Financial Crises one questions loomed large. What the hell is Money. Why does it do the things it does. To doublelucky productions, the answer was clear. Money is a living creature with an almost unstoppable will to live. A super creature using Humans as its host to conquer more and more "lebensraum." From here it was easy to make the next step. Money was an Alien from outer space.  This lecture performance tried to explain the facts. Sci-fi films are the only known source of reliable information about Aliens. With a handful of Movie clips, interviews with economists, and a heavy dose of speculation, doublelucky productions reveals the horrifying and mind-boggling truth about Money: It Came From Outer Space.
